Strava: Identify Critical Funnels

Product Analytics | Funnels | Competitive Research | Market Research | Product Strategy

Overview

This analysis identified and optimized critical user funnels at Strava, focusing on key conversion points for both athletes and business partners. The goal was to figure out key value funnels that drive business and customer success.

Key Findings

  • Product Analytics: Analyzed user behavior data to identify funnel bottlenecks
  • Funnel Optimization: Designed improvements for critical conversion paths
  • Competitive Research: Benchmarked against industry competitors
  • Market Research: Understood athlete and partner needs
  • Product Strategy: Aligned funnel improvements with business goals

Outcomes

Identified key value drivers for both athletes and business partners, enabling targeted improvements to platform funnels and user experience.
