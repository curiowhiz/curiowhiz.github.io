Google News: User Growth

Market Research | Problem Prioritization | Product Roadmap | Product Strategy | Wireframes

Overview

This project focused on driving user growth and engagement at Google News through data-driven strategies, market research, and strategic product planning. The goal was to identify opportunities for user acquisition and retention.

Approach

  • Market Research: Understanding user needs and competitive landscape
  • Problem Prioritization: Identifying top barriers to user engagement
  • Product Roadmap: Strategic planning for feature releases
  • Product Strategy: Long-term vision for user growth
  • Wireframes: Designing improved user interfaces

Impact

Boosted user engagement at Google News through targeted feature improvements and strategic product decisions focused on delivering user value.
