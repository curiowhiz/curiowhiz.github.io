Google News: User Growth

Driving user engagement and growth through market research, problem prioritization, and strategic product roadmapping.

📊 View Full Case Study → ← Back to Work
Type Product Case Study
Role Growth PM
Methods Market Research, Roadmapping
Outcome +25% Target User Growth
🎯

The Problem

Google News faced low retention among casual readers, weak personalization, competitive pressure from aggregators, mobile friction, and declining trust among younger demographics. Growth was stalling without a strategic intervention.

🔍

The Approach

Conducted market analysis (mobile-first preference, AI-driven recommendations), user behavior analysis (8-12 min sessions, drop-off after 3 articles), competitor benchmarking, and demographic insights (45% aged 18-34, 72% mobile traffic).

📋

Prioritization Framework

Priority 1: Personalization algorithm to increase session duration. Priority 2: Mobile onboarding optimization to reduce first-week churn. Priority 3: Push notification strategy for DAU growth.

✨

Quarterly Roadmap

Q1: Enhanced recommendations + A/B testing. Q2: Topic subscriptions + save-for-later. Q3: Social features + international expansion. Q4: Premium tier + smart digest emails.

Want to dive deeper?

  • Low retention rates among casual news readers
  • Lack of personalization in news feed recommendations
  • Competitive pressure from other news aggregation platforms
  • Mobile user experience friction points
  • Declining trust in news sources among younger demographics
  • Market Trends: Growing preference for personalized news feeds and mobile-first experiences
  • User Behavior: Users spend average 8-12 minutes per session; drop-off occurs after 3 articles
  • Competitor Analysis: Key competitors focusing on AI-driven recommendations and community features
  • Demographic Insights: 45% of users are aged 18-34; mobile accounts for 72% of traffic
  • Q1: Enhance recommendation algorithm; A/B test personalization features
  • Q2: Launch topic-based subscriptions; implement save-for-later functionality
  • Q3: Introduce social sharing features; expand international coverage
  • Q4: Launch premium features; implement smart digest emails
  • User Acquisition: +25% new user growth YoY
  • Retention: Improve 30-day retention from 35% to 50%
  • Engagement: Increase average session time from 10 to 15 minutes
  • Daily Active Users: +30% DAU growth
