Groww: Cut Abandonment Levels

Competitive Research | Hypothesis Testing | Market Research | User Research

Overview

This project tackled a critical challenge: reducing user abandonment in the Groww investment app. Through rigorous user research and competitive analysis, we identified key friction points and designed solutions to improve user retention.

Methodology

  • User Research: Conducted interviews and surveys to understand abandonment patterns
  • Competitive Research: Analyzed competing fintech solutions to identify best practices
  • Market Research: Studied market trends in investment app engagement
  • Hypothesis Testing: Validated assumptions through A/B testing and experimentation

Results

Successfully identified and addressed key abandonment drivers, resulting in improved user retention and engagement metrics across the platform.
