Groww: Cut Abandonment Levels

Reducing user abandonment in the investment app through user research, competitive analysis, and hypothesis-driven testing.

📊 View Full Case Study → ← Back to Work
Type Product Case Study
Role PM Analysis
Methods User Research, A/B Testing
Outcome 40% Abandonment Reduction
🎯

The Problem

Groww faced high abandonment: 35% during KYC, 28% in first investment, 22% in fund selection, 18% at payment. Users — especially first-time investors — were dropping off due to complexity, choice paralysis, and trust gaps.

🔍

The Approach

Conducted 25+ user interviews, 500+ surveys, heatmap analysis, and benchmarked 8 competing fintech platforms. Identified KYC complexity, choice paralysis, trust gaps, and mobile friction as root causes.

⚙️

Hypothesis Testing Results

Three hypotheses tested through A/B experiments — all validated with statistically significant improvements.

hypothesis │ test_type │ result
simplified_kyc A/B test +32%
curated_funds A/B test +40%
education_inline A/B test +22%
✨

The Outcome

40% decrease in overall abandonment. 35% increase in completed first investments. Onboarding time reduced from 18 to 8 minutes. NPS improved from 32 to 54 points.

Want to dive deeper?

User Research Approach:

  • Conducted 25+ user interviews with abandoned and active users
  • Analyzed user behavior through heatmaps and session recordings
  • Surveyed 500+ users on friction points and pain areas
  • Tracked drop-off patterns across mobile and web platforms

Competitive Research:

  • Analyzed onboarding flows of 8 competing fintech platforms
  • Benchmarked conversion rates and abandonment metrics
  • Identified best practices in KYC and verification
  • KYC Complexity: 12 minute average; users unfamiliar with financial terms abandoned at 2x rate
  • Choice Paralysis: Users with more than 20 fund recommendations abandoned
  • Trust & Education Gap: First-time investors needed more education before committing
  • Mobile Friction: 60% of abandonment occurred on mobile due to small forms and difficult uploads
  • Simplified KYC: Redesigned 6-step process into 3 clear steps with progress indicator
  • Smart Fund Recommendations: AI-powered curation showing top 5 funds
  • In-app Education: Tooltips, video tutorials, and glossary
  • Mobile Optimization: Larger forms, camera document upload
  • Progress Saving: Resume later without re-entering data
  • Security Reassurance: Clear data privacy communication
  • Simplicity beats features — removing 50% of steps improved outcomes
  • User education is critical for financial products
  • Mobile-first design is essential for fintech apps
  • Hypothesis-driven testing validates assumptions before full implementation
  • Understanding user psychology drives better product decisions
