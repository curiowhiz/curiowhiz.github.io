Spotify: Event Booking Experience

Streamlining a broken event discovery and ticketing journey through user research, friction analysis, and RICE-prioritized solutions to improve user retention by 10%.

The Problem

Spotify's event booking journey suffered from 42% abandonment, 8-minute average completion time (vs. 3-4 min benchmark), and disconnected discovery — losing ticket revenue and missing opportunities to deepen user engagement with artists and live content.

🔍

The Approach

Conducted 12 user interviews and tested wireframes with 25 users across three segments (Concert Enthusiasts, Casual Listeners, Explorers). Mapped journeys, identified friction points, and applied the RICE framework to prioritize highest-impact solutions.

⚙️

RICE Prioritization Matrix

Evaluated four candidate features against Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort to focus engineering on highest-leverage opportunities first.

The Outcome

Projected 64% reduction in booking abandonment (42% → 15%), 75% faster checkout (8 min → 2 min), 3x more events discovered per session, and an estimated 25% lift in ticket sales — all driving a 10% improvement in 12-month retention.

Want to dive deeper?

User Research:

  • 12 user interviews across Concert Enthusiasts, Casual Listeners, and Explorers
  • Wireframe testing with 25 users to validate friction-reduction hypotheses
  • Task completion rate measurement before/after design changes
  • Qualitative feedback synthesis on pain points and proposed solutions

Journey Mapping:

  • End-to-end maps across all three segments with friction overlays
  • Ranked top 5 friction points by % of users affected
  • Unclear event discovery path — affects 45% of users
  • Multi-step checkout process — affects 38% of users
  • Unexpected fee surprises — affects 35% of users
  • No social context about attendees — affects 28% of users
  • Limited artist info at decision point — affects 22% of users
  • Personalized Event Discovery: ML-driven recommendations from listening history and attendance patterns
  • One-Click Checkout: Saved payment + preferences, 8 min → 2 min
  • Social Integration: Friend attendance signals for group planning
  • Transparent Pricing: Full fee breakdown surfaced before payment
  • Mobile-Optimized Flow: Touch-friendly UI for on-the-go booking
  • Smart Notifications: Tied to listening habits and artist activity
  • Friction compounds — small frictions create exponential abandonment
  • Personalization meaningfully drives engagement and conversion
  • Transparency builds trust and reduces decision paralysis
  • RICE prevents waste by focusing effort on highest-impact opportunities
  • Social context dramatically increases event adoption
  • Mobile-first is non-negotiable for event discovery and booking
