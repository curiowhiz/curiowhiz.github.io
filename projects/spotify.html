Spotify: Event Booking Experience

Streamlining a broken event discovery and ticketing journey through user research, friction analysis, and RICE-prioritized solutions to improve user retention by 10%.

User Research RICE Framework Retention Optimization Friction Analysis Journey Mapping

Project Overview

This case study showcases my end-to-end product management approach to solving a critical user engagement challenge at Spotify. Through user research, friction analysis, strategic prioritization, and solution design, I identified and addressed key barriers in the event booking journey that were preventing users from discovering and attending live events.

Challenge: Users struggled to discover events, navigate the booking process, and complete purchases, resulting in low conversion rates and missed engagement opportunities.

Solution: Identify friction points through user research, prioritize opportunities using RICE framework, and design a streamlined ticketing experience that reduces abandonment and improves user retention.

The Problem: Broken Event Booking Journey

Problem Statement:

Spotify's event discovery and booking experience creates unnecessary friction at multiple touchpoints, causing users to abandon the journey and miss opportunities to deepen their engagement with artists and live content.

User Research Findings

Through user interviews, usability testing, and behavior analysis, I identified several critical pain points:

  • Poor Event Discoverability: Users struggle to find events matching their music preferences and location
  • Complex Booking Flow: Multi-step process with unclear CTAs and redundant information requests
  • Lack of Personalization: Event recommendations don't align with listening history or favorite artists
  • Payment Friction: Unexpected fees, unclear pricing breakdown, and multiple payment confirmations
  • Missed Context: Limited artist information and event details at critical decision moments
  • No Social Proof: Absence of friend attendance indicators and community engagement signals

Impact on User Engagement

📉 Booking Abandonment Rate: 42% of users abandon the booking journey before payment completion
😤 User Frustration: "Finding and booking events feels disconnected from my music experience"
💰 Revenue Impact: Missed ticket sales and lost opportunity to deepen user lifetime value
⏱️ Time-to-Complete: Average 8-minute booking flow (industry benchmark: 3-4 minutes)

Solution: Streamlined Event Booking Experience

✅ Solution Overview:

A redesigned event discovery and booking journey that personalizes recommendations, reduces friction at key touchpoints, and leverages social signals to increase conversion rates and user retention.

RICE Framework Prioritization

I applied the RICE framework (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort) to evaluate and prioritize opportunities:

🎯 Personalized Event Feed: Reach: 60% | Impact: High (4x) | Confidence: 80% | Effort: 4 weeks = RICE Score: 48
⚡ One-Click Booking: Reach: 80% | Impact: High (3x) | Confidence: 90% | Effort: 3 weeks = RICE Score: 72 (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
👥 Friend Attendance Signals: Reach: 45% | Impact: High (2x) | Confidence: 75% | Effort: 2 weeks = RICE Score: 33.75
🎫 Transparent Pricing: Reach: 75% | Impact: High (3x) | Confidence: 85% | Effort: 1 week = RICE Score: 191.25 (QUICK WIN)

Core Features Designed

🎵 Personalized Event Discovery: Machine-learning-driven event recommendations based on favorite artists, genres, and attendance history
⚡ One-Click Checkout: Streamlined payment flow with saved preferences, reducing booking time from 8 minutes to 2 minutes
💬 Social Integration: Display which friends are attending, enabling group planning and social motivation
💰 Transparent Pricing Display: Clear breakdown of ticket price, service fees, and taxes upfront before payment
📱 Mobile-Optimized Experience: Touch-friendly interface optimized for on-the-go event discovery and booking
🔔 Smart Notifications: Timely reminders for upcoming events and artist announcements tied to user's listening habits

Friction Analysis Process

Journey Mapping

I conducted detailed journey mapping across three user segments:

  • Concert Enthusiasts: Frequent attendees seeking new experiences (priority: discovery)
  • Casual Listeners: Occasional attendees discovering through artist recommendations (priority: frictionless checkout)
  • Explorers: New to events, seeking social validation and clear information (priority: social proof & transparency)

Friction Point Identification

Top 5 Friction Points (Ranked by Impact):

1. Unclear event discovery path (45% of users)
2. Multi-step checkout process (38% of users)
3. Unexpected fee surprises (35% of users)
4. No social context about attendees (28% of users)
5. Limited artist information at decision point (22% of users)

User Testing Validation

  • Conducted 12 user interviews across different segments
  • Tested wireframes with 25 users to validate usability improvements
  • Measured task completion rates before/after design changes
  • Gathered qualitative feedback on pain points and solutions

Design & Prototyping

Wireframe Development

I created detailed wireframes for each stage of the booking journey, focusing on reducing cognitive load and decision friction:

📍 Discovery Screen: Personalized event grid with artist photos, venue info, and friend attendance indicators
ℹ️ Event Detail Screen: Complete event information, artist bio, venue details, seating map, and transparent pricing
🛒 Checkout Screen: One-page checkout with saved payment methods, clear fee breakdown, and social sharing prompts
✅ Confirmation Screen: Immediate booking confirmation with ticket delivery options and calendar integration

Interactive Prototype

Built a high-fidelity prototype demonstrating the end-to-end user experience, including:

  • Dynamic event recommendations based on listening history
  • Smooth transitions between discovery, detail, and checkout flows
  • Real-time fee calculations with transparent breakdown
  • Social features showing friend attendance in real-time

Expected Impact

📈 User Retention: Improve 12-month retention by 10% through increased event engagement and community connection
🎫 Booking Conversion: Reduce abandonment from 42% to 15% through streamlined checkout (-64%)
⏱️ Time-to-Complete: Reduce average booking time from 8 minutes to 2 minutes (-75%)
🎵 Event Discoverability: Increase events discovered per user session by 3x through personalization
💰 Revenue Impact: Estimated 25% increase in ticket sales through improved conversion rates
👥 Social Engagement: 60% of bookings expected to include social sharing or friend invitations

📊 Friction Analysis: Systematically identified, prioritized, and validated pain points in the booking experience
🎯 RICE Prioritization: Used RICE framework to evaluate opportunities and focus on highest-impact solutions
🎨 Solution Design: Created comprehensive design solutions addressing multiple friction points
📱 UX/UI Thinking: Designed mobile-first experience optimized for quick decision-making and conversion
📈 Metrics & Impact: Quantified potential impact of proposed solutions using data-driven projections

Key Learnings

  • Friction Compounds: Multiple small friction points create exponential abandonment—focus on cumulative reduction
  • Personalization Drives Engagement: Tailored experiences significantly increase user motivation and conversion
  • Transparency Builds Trust: Clear pricing and information reduces anxiety and decision paralysis
  • RICE Prevents Waste: Structured prioritization ensures effort focuses on highest-impact opportunities
  • Social Context Matters: Community and friend engagement signals dramatically increase event adoption
  • Mobile-First is Essential: Event discovery and booking happens primarily on mobile—design accordingly
