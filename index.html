About Me

I'm a data-driven Product Manager with a passion for solving complex user problems and building products that drive measurable business impact. With experience across high-growth tech companies (Google, Strava, Groww), I excel at translating user insights into scalable product strategy, rapid iteration, and retention-focused growth initiatives.

My expertise spans Product Strategy Development, User Research & Discovery, Data-Driven Analytics, Roadmapping & Prioritization, Cross-Functional Leadership, and Agile Methodologies. I'm skilled in identifying critical friction points through journey mapping, prioritizing opportunities using frameworks like RICE, designing intuitive wireframes, and leveraging metrics to validate hypotheses—all while maintaining a relentless focus on user outcomes and business KPIs.

Recently, I led initiatives that improved user retention by 10% at Spotify through friction analysis and streamlined UX, accelerated user growth at Google News via strategic market research and prioritization, and optimized critical conversion funnels at Strava by identifying key value drivers. I'm comfortable working with engineering teams, design partners, and stakeholders to ship products on time and iterate based on user feedback.
