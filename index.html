About Me

I'm a data-driven Product Manager with a passion for solving complex user problems and building products that drive measurable business impact. Through deep-dive product case studies across Google, Strava, Groww, and Spotify, I've developed expertise in translating user insights into scalable product strategy, rapid iteration, and retention-focused growth initiatives. I specialize in leveraging AI-powered tools like Claude AI, Google AI Studio, and n8n automation to accelerate research, scale user discovery, validate hypotheses, and bring ideas to market faster than traditional approaches.

My expertise spans Product Strategy Development, User Research and Discovery, Data-Driven Analytics, Roadmapping and Prioritization, Cross-Functional Leadership, and Agile Methodologies. I'm skilled in identifying critical friction points through journey mapping, prioritizing opportunities using frameworks like RICE, designing intuitive wireframes, and leveraging metrics to validate hypotheses, all while maintaining a relentless focus on user outcomes and business KPIs. I'm proficient with a modern PM tech stack including Claude AI for research synthesis, problem framing and insight generation, Google AI Studio for rapid prototyping, AI-powered pattern recognition and hypothesis validation, Figma for collaborative design and interactive prototyping, and n8n for automating research workflows, scaling data collection and eliminating manual research bottlenecks. This enables me to execute research at enterprise scale and validate ideas 3x faster than traditional approaches.

Through detailed case studies, I've improved user retention by 10% at Spotify through AI-assisted friction analysis and streamlined UX design, accelerated user growth at Google News via strategic market research and prioritization, and optimized critical conversion funnels at Strava by identifying key value drivers. I'm comfortable working with engineering teams, design partners, and stakeholders to ship products on time and iterate based on user feedback. I'm equally comfortable prototyping low-fidelity concepts, running AI-powered user research synthesis with Claude, automating repetitive research processes with n8n, or building rapid interactive prototypes in Figma to uncover deeper insights and validate product direction faster.
